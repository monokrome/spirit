## Theological Context

### Angelic Traditions
Angels appear across Abrahamic religions with varying names and hierarchies:
- **Judaism**: Malakhim, with named angels in later texts
- **Christianity**: Elaborate hierarchy developed by church fathers
- **Islam**: Malā'ika, including Jibril (Gabriel) and Mīkā'īl (Michael)

### Ascended Masters
The concept developed through:
1. **Theosophy** (1875+): Mahatmas or Masters of Wisdom
2. **I AM Activity** (1930s): Ascended Master teachings
3. **New Age movement**: Expanded roster of masters

### Nature Spirits
Cross-cultural belief in nature spirits:
- Celtic: Aos Sí, Fair Folk
- Germanic: Elves, Dwarves
- Slavic: Leshy, Vodyanoy
- Japanese: Kami, Yokai

## Working with These Frequencies

Practitioners suggest:
- Use angelic frequencies during prayer or meditation
- Ascended master frequencies during study of their teachings
- Fae frequencies in nature or garden settings
- Approach with respect and clear intention

The frequencies are modern associations and should be understood as tools for focus rather than literal vibrational signatures of these beings.
